Frank Gehry Architecture Presentation

Guggenheim Bilbao

The Guggenheim Museum Bilbao is a modern and contemporary art museum, designed by the Canadian-American architect Frank Gehry. It opened in 1997.

Walt Disney Concert Hall

The Walt Disney Concert Hall is the home of the Los Angeles Philharmonic. Its striking design and remarkable acoustics make it a landmark.

MIT Stata Center

The Ray and Maria Stata Center is the heart of MIT’s innovation, designed to represent a collaborative environment.

Experience Music Project

The Experience Music Project is a museum dedicated to contemporary popular culture, located in Seattle, Washington.

Louis Vuitton Foundation

The Louis Vuitton Foundation is a museum and cultural center in Paris, nestled in the Bois de Boulogne.

IAC Building

The IAC Building is home to the media company IAC, known for its innovative design and structure.

Furniture Designs

Frank Gehry has also influenced furniture design with unique pieces that echo his architectural style.
